# Hippo Types

Helping elementary aged students with dyslexia increase confidence in typing

Group 1: Hungry Hungry Hippos

# Our Mission

How might we help elementary-age learners manage the impact of dyslexia on their ability to use a traditional QWERTY-based keyboard?

We focused on the UN SDG Goal 4: Quality Education, specifically with the goals of aiding the SDG of “by 2030, ensure(ing) that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults, both men and women, achieve literacy and numeracy.” Through implementing multisensory features tailored for dyslexic learners with specific training modules to improve typing abilities, we aim to reduce barriers of educational access, accessibility issues, and improve confidence through creating customized aid empowered through equitable design.
